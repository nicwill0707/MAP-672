Kentucky cities, large and small

Ever visited these great Central Kentucky locations?

Map authored by Nic Williamson

If the daily grind has got you down, skip town! There are lots of great cities and towns in Kentucky, and they are all very accessible for an afternoon drive or even a long distance bicycling trip. How about try catching up on some history in the state capital, Frankfort? Or grab your long rifle for some deerhunting at the WMA in Stamping Ground! Need to kick back? Go bar hopping around the many breweries popping up in Lexington. You are sure to find something to fit your mood in the Central Bluegrass.
